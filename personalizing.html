Personalizing the Urn

All of the urns made by Blue Ridge Vessels are hand-crafted and are custom-made for your pet.

The urns are available in four sizes:

Petite Keepsake - 2.5"x2.5"x2.5": up to 10 lbs $85

Medium - 6.5"x6.5"x6.5": up to 100 lbs $269

Small - 4.5"x4.5"x4.5":  up to 40 lbs $140

Individual - 9"x9"x9": up to 220 lbs $415

Personalize your pet's urn with a name and dates

Make your pet's urn even more unique by adding his or her name and dates to the urn. Patti will carve your pet's personalization on the bottom of the urn. Choose this personalization option below, if desired. If adding dates too, please type the date range exactly as you would like it to be carved. For example just as numbers 6/24/99 - 5/30/10, as long dates June 24, 1999 - May 30, 2010, or as abbreviated dates Jun. 24, 1999 - May 30, 2010. Due to space, only a name can be carved on the Petite Keepsake Size. PLEASE NOTE: The nature of the hand made is that each piece is unique and will have slight variations of color, surface and size.
